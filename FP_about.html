About

I grew up in Kyoto, Japan. Although I liked making art with my bare hands, art wasn't necessarily important in my life until I took art classes in college.
I learned drawing skills and I realized I could draw well. I also learned new ways to make art, which led to my interest in making art using Photoshop, etc.
Therefore, I make different types of art, but my focus is graphic design.
I usually incorporate hand drawing and computer design using Photoshop and Illustration.
I like making simple designs. I do not like art that needs complicated visual elements and hidden meanings in order to understand art.
Therefore, my art is simple and easy to understand. I want to make people smile. So I like to incorporate humor to my art.
Being Japanese, I have an appreciation for the ordinary fixtures of life. I find something lovely in every little thing and make my art lovely.
